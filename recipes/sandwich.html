Sandwich

Description

Sandwich - Two slices of bread with some air between them. If you fancy you can replace the air with some ham and cheese

Pro tip - For easier ingestion the inside of the sandwich can be lubricated with ketchup or (and) mayo.

Ingredients

  • Bread - Sliced
  • Air
  • (Optional) Ketchup, mayo
  • (Optional) Ham
  • (Optional) Cheese

Steps

  1. Put two slices of bread on a plate
  2. If you go for the basic version, quickly close the slices together to get as much air in as possible
  3. For advanced version before closing the bread spread some ketchup, mayo on the bread
  4. Add sliced ham and cheese
  5. Close the bread
  6. Check if you closed the sandwich correctly (Ingredients on the inside, bread on the outside)
  7. Question your sanity
  8. Do a little dance
  9. Enjoy your sandwich!
