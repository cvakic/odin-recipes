Rice

Description

Boiled Rice. You can eat it as is, add some sauce, or fry it with some eggs or something.

Universal ingredient for many tasty foods. You should always have some rice in your pantry.

Ingredients

  • Dry rice
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Butter (optional)

Steps

  1. (Butter Version) Put some butter in a pot and start the heat
  2. When the butter is dissolved add dry rice
  3. Stir the rice and let the butter soak in it
  4. Add water (for 1 cup of rice add 1 1/2 cups of water)
  5. Add salt
  6. Lower the heat and cover the pot with a lid
  7. Boil for around 10-15 minutes or until the water is soaked in rice
  8. Fluff the rice with a fork and cover for 10 minutes
  9. Enjoy your rice!
