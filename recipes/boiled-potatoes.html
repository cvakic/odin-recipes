Boiled Potatoes

Description

Boiled potatoes are very easy to make and packed with nutrients we all need.

Feel free to add ingredients you like for your satisfaction.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Cummin
  • Butter (optional)

Steps

  1. Peel your potatoes
  2. Quarter the potatoes and put them in a pot
  3. Add water to the pot
  4. Bring the water to boil and add the salt and cummin
  5. Cook for 20-25 minutes until the potatoes are soft
  6. Drain the water and put the potatoes on a plate
  7. (Optional) Put some butter on your potatoes and stir a little
  8. Enjoy your potatoes!
