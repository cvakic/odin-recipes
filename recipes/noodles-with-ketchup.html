Noodles with ketchup

Description

We all come home sometimes and look in our empty fridges looking for something edible.

If you have a pack of pasta and some ketchup left this is a recipe for you.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (any shape you have, we go with noodles)
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Ketchup
  • Hot Dogs (optional)
  • Shredded cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil some water in a pot
  2. Add salt in the water
  3. Add pasta of your choice and boil until soft
  4. Rinse the pasta and put it in a bowl
  5. Put some ketchup in the bowl of pasta
  6. If you're fancy you can add the hot dogs and cheese
  7. Stir the noodles
  8. (Optional) Put in a microwave for 2 minutes or in oven for 10 minutes for the noodles to soak in the sauce
  9. Enjoy your noodles!
